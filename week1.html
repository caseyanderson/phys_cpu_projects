LAB: The Command Line

Navigate to the Command Line on Mac OSX (Applications > Utilities > Terminal).
You should see something that looks like the white background window on the right of the image. That is the Terminal, a name for a command line interface that is already installed on your computer. Though other aspects of computers have changed drastically over the last fifty years, this has not. In other words, you can see back in time, in a sense, at this level of your computer.
