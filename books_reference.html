Practical Electronics for Inventors, if there is one and only one book that you purchase to help you learn electronics it should be this one. The Art of Electronics, one of the most comprehensive books on electronics I am aware of. Possibly overkill for our needs, but a good resource nonetheless (also $$$$$). Handmade Electronic Music: The Art of Hardware Hacking, an excellent book covering the wonderful world of DIY audio technology for work with sound and music. Also functions as a good introductory electronics book.
